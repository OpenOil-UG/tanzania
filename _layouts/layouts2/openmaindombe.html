Open Mai Ndombe

Welcome to Open Mai Ndombe

This portal brings together REDD+ and FLEG independent observer reporting for the Mai Ndombe jurisdiction. It features interactive mapping stories that summarize the results of recent or ongoing monitoring missions to the district. It also holds reports on IM methodolgy development conducted by Moabi and our partners as well as a range of maps provided through our consortium. We hope the portal provides a more detailed view of on the ground monitoring activities and sparks discussion regarding strengthening forest governance efforts.

Why Open Mai Ndombe?

As the location ER-Reduction program, Mai Ndombe is at the heart of DRC's nascent REDD+ program. There are three REDD+ pilot projects, 20 logging concessions, and numerous customary land rights many of which overlap with these activities. Harmonizing IMREDD and FLEG.
